# MyPhone Remote 📱🖥

**Your iPhone. Your Mac. One touch.**

MyPhone Remote turns your iPhone into a wireless remote control for your Mac. No App Store download. No account creation. Just open Safari and go.

## 🔋 Free Features
- **Screen Mirror**: See your Mac desktop on your iPhone in real time
- **Tap to Click**: Touch your iPhone screen to click anywhere on your Mac
- **Basic Hotkeys**: Spotlight, Mission Control, app switching

## 💎 Pro Features
- **Full Gesture Control**: Drag, scroll, pinch-to-zoom, right-click
- **Complete Hotkey Panel**: 30+ keyboard shortcuts at your fingertips
- **D-Pad Navigation**: Arrow keys + Enter/Esc for precise control
- **Annotation Overlay**: Draw on screen with pencil and highlighter
- **Double-Tap Double-Click**: Open files and apps the iPhone way

## 🤖 AI Agent Features
- **AI Vision API**: Let AI agents see your screen in real time
- **Voice Bridge**: Speak through your iPhone, hear your Mac respond
- **Multi-Agent Vision**: Multiple AI agents share the same screen view
- **Annotation → AI Pipeline**: Your drawings become AI instructions

## 🛡 Privacy First
- **100% Local**: All traffic stays on your WiFi. Nothing touches the cloud.
- **No Account Required**: Open the URL. That's it.
- **Open Source Server**: Inspect the code yourself.

## 📱 How It Works
1. Run MyPhone Remote on your Mac (one command)
2. Open Safari on your iPhone
3. Type the URL shown in terminal
4. You're in control. That's it.

No companion app. No Bluetooth pairing. No USB cable. Just WiFi.
