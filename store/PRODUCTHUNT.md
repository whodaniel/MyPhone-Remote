# MyPhone Remote — ProductHunt Launch Copy

## Tagline
Your iPhone becomes a wireless remote control for your Mac. No App Store. No account. Just Safari.

## Description

MyPhone Remote turns your iPhone into a wireless trackpad, keyboard, and screen mirror for your Mac — with zero friction.

**No companion iOS app.** No App Store download. No Bluetooth pairing. No USB cable. No account creation.

You run one command on your Mac, open a URL on your iPhone, and you're in control. That's it.

### What you get:

🖥️ **Live Screen Mirror** — See your Mac desktop on your iPhone in real time with adaptive quality that stays smooth even on older hardware.

👆 **Touch Control** — Tap to click, double-tap to double-click, drag to move, pinch to zoom. Every iPhone gesture maps naturally to your Mac.

⌨️ **30+ Hotkeys** — Spotlight, Mission Control, Cmd+Tab app switching, arrow key D-pad — all from your phone screen.

✏️ **Annotation Overlay** — Draw on your screen with pencil and highlighter tools. Perfect for presentations and walkthroughs.

🤖 **AI Vision (Pro+)** — Let AI agents see your screen in real time. Multiple agents share the same desktop view simultaneously. Voice bridge for bidirectional STT/TTS.

### Why this is different:

Every remote desktop app wants you to install a companion iOS app, create an account, and pay $15/month for cloud relay servers. MyPhone Remote runs a tiny local server on your Mac — your data never leaves your WiFi. The free tier is genuinely free forever.

Built for the AI era: the AI Agent tier gives any vision-capable AI direct access to read your screen and respond to what you're doing. This is multi-agent shared vision — the same desktop, seen by Hermes, Gemini, Claude, or any agent that can fetch a JPEG.

### Pricing:
- **Free** — Mirror + tap + basic hotkeys
- **Pro** ($4.99/mo) — Full gestures, all hotkeys, D-pad, annotations
- **AI Agent** ($14.99/mo) — Everything + AI vision API, voice bridge, multi-agent
